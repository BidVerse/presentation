By Jacob Zwang, Ethan Hopkins, Ben Swerdlow, and a little bit of Jason Telanoff

The Problem

Current industry standards encourage contractors for work for the cheapest possible, creating rushed, low quality work.

Our Product

Our product creates the opposite. BidVerse allows companies to bid on contractors time. This helps contractors get the best value from their time, and increase the value companies get from their contractors.
